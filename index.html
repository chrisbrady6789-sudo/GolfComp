⛳ Competition Rules - US Open 2026

  • Pick 1 golfer from each of the 8 brackets (brackets based on odds for top 64 players) to form your team of 8.
  • Your team score is calculated from the best 4 scores out of 8 (relative to par). The lowest combined score wins.
  • If a golfer withdraws before the start, you will be given the golfer below in the bracket (or the top golfer if at the bottom). You can also choose another golfer in that bracket before the deadline.
  • If a golfer withdraws or is disqualified during the tournament, a score of +20 is recorded for the entire competition.
  • If a golfer misses the cut, they receive +8 for each missed round (+16 total) added to their 2-round score.
  • An extra -3 bonus is applied to the golfer who wins the US Open.
  • If 2 competitors are tied on score, all 8 golfers on their team count to break the tie.
  • Only one entry per person — entrants must be over 18 as at 01 Jan 2026.
  • The entry fee is €5 — please Revolut Fran the entry fee when submitting your team.
  • Prize money and number of prizes will depend on the number of entrants and will be communicated before competition starts.
  • Picks must be submitted before 10am on Thursday 18th June 2026.
